Learning & Evolved

The complexity of technology landscapes are straining traditional learning approaches.

How can learners prepare for a future where the only constant is change? How do educators keep up? And how can employers predict adaptability of prospective hires?

We belive the answer requires bold new approaches to learning, and thats why we created Nebula.

Get Inspiered and Stay Curious

Learning How to Learn

Effective learning will be the keystone skill of future career professionals. We focus on developing the core future skills that make us uniqely human, including creative adaptive problem solving, collaboration & communication, logical thinking, synthesis and leadership.

Embrace Complexity

As technologies and markets continue to collide, learners will need to put adaptability front-and-center. Our platform is designed to embrace non-linear thinking by leveraging approaches developed by the complexity sciences community.

Learn by Doing

Project-based learning not only provides context and motivation for learners, it also produces tangible outcomes that can be shared easily to prospective employers. We belive that true learning doesnt begin until action is taken.

Build Community

If you want to go fast, go alone. If you want to go far, go together. We have build our platform to make learning and sharing with others a seamless and fun experience. With built-in incentive structures and access to expert mentors, we let you share your biggest wins, but also help keep you on track.
